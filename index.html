I'm a Lecturer & Researcher

Noreena Yi-Chin Liu is a Lecturer in Design and Creative Industries in the faculty of Arts and Social Science at University of Brunei Darussalam. Her research focuses on using games as interactive tools for health and education purposes.

Topic: Immersive technologies, Augmented Reality, Gamification, Serious Games, Human Computer Interaction, Advertising, Cultural and Creative Industries, Public Health, Mental Health
